Hospitality Background

I've spent 15 years working in restaurants, bars and cafes. I've worked almost every position you can in a restaurant. Chef, barista, bartender, host, server, and general manager. After I went to culinary school, I started out cooking in restaurants and did that for many years. I was able to work my way up to sous chef and manage a kitchen.

After cooking for a few years I decided to get into coffee. Out of all the jobs that I have worked, I loved being a barista the most. I like the balance between creativity and being social. Although I don't cook professionally anymore, I still love to cook at home and make delicious meals. My favorite things to cook and eat are,

  • Pizza
  • Ramen
  • Pasta
  • Anything grilled

Leather Work

For the past 7 years I have been working with leather on the side. It brings me so much joy to be able to take something like a simple side of leather and turn it into a wallet or bag.

I was first inspired to start making leather goods after a trip to Portland Oregon. The shop I visited had great wallets and accessories that inspired me to try and create one of my own. Since then, I have been able to study abroad with a great leather craftsman in Japan, where I learned to hone my craft.
